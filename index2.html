The Princess & the Bodyguard discovered their true identity one cold day in New York. A gentleman approached them, looked them up and down, taking in all the fine details, including, importantly, that the Bodyguard was carrying a mandolin. He stopped - he paused - and said "You Princess, go there " as he motioned towards a yellow taxi. "You, Bodyguard, go with her". And in that moment, the Princess & the Bodyguard knew their true identities.
